Hello {primary_applicant_name},

You have an upcoming hearing proceeding and we need to know if you still need your dispute resolution hearing. If you have already resolved your issue(s), moved out, or no longer require the hearing, you have the option to withdraw the hearing and receive a refund of your $100 filing fee.

Important: A response is required

Please respond to this email along with your instruction to "proceed with my hearing" or "withdraw my hearing" in the body of the response. Alternatively, you can call the Residential Tenancy Branch information line at 1-800-665-8779 to ask for a callback from the Hearing Verification team.

If you give us instructions to withdraw the hearing, we will contact your landlord to obtain their consent to withdraw the hearing as this is an application to cancel a Notice to End Tenancy. Alternatively, you can also attach a written letter signed by your landlord agreeing to the withdrawal with your response.

Withdrawal: $100 filing fee will be refunded

When your dispute is withdrawn, if you paid a filing fee, your $100 filing fee will be refunded. An email confirmation will be sent once the hearing is withdrawn.

Please provide the information of your method of payment:

  • Online Payment - Please provide the card type (mastercard, visa, amex etc.) and the last 4 digits of the card. The refund will be returned to your credit card in approximately 5-10 business days.
  • Office Payment - Please provide your mailing address. The refund will be mailed to you by cheque to you from the Ministry of Finance in approximately 6-8 weeks. If the payment was made by credit card, please also provide the card type (mastercard, visa, amex etc.) and the last 4 digits of the card.
